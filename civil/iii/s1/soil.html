Chapter - 3

Weight
$$\displaylines{W=W_w+W_s=\omega W_s+W_s=W_s(\omega +1)}$$

Volume
$$\displaylines{V=V_v+V_s}$$

Void ratio
$$\displaylines{e={V_v\over V_s}\\e=\frac{n}{1-n}\\ e=\omega_{\textsf{sat}} G_s\;(\because S=100\%=1)}$$

Porosity
$$n={V_v\over V}\quad n=\frac{e}{1+e}$$

Degree of Saturation
$$S={{V_w\over V_v}\quad S=\frac{\omega G_s}{e}}$$

Water content
$$\omega={W_w\over W_s}$$

Unit Weight
$$\gamma={W\over V}\quad V_w=\frac{W_w}{\gamma_w}\quad\gamma={(1+\omega)G_s\gamma_w\over 1+e}$$

Dry Unit Weight
$$\gamma_d={W_s\over V}\quad\gamma_d={\gamma\over 1+\omega}\quad\gamma_d={G_s\gamma_w\over 1+e}$$

Relationship Equation
$$\displaylines{W_s=G_sV_s\gamma_w\quad G_s=\frac{W_s}{V_s\gamma_w}\\V_s=1\rightarrow W_s=G_s\gamma_w\\\gamma_w=9.81\;kN/m^3,\;62.4\;lb/ft^3}$$

Saturated Density
$$\displaylines{\rho_{\textsf{sat}}=\frac{(G_s+e)\rho_w}{1+e}\\\textsf{Mass of water to be added}=\rho_{\textsf{sat}}-\rho}$$

Note
$$\displaylines{\gamma\textsf{ and }\rho\textsf{ interchanges in some equations.}}$$

Chapter - 4

Shrinkage Limit
$$\displaylines{SL=\omega_i(\%)-\Delta\omega (\%)\\\omega_i(\%)=\frac{M_1-M_2}{M_2}\times 100\\\Delta\omega (\%)=\frac{(V_i-V_f)\rho_w}{M_2}\times 100\\SL=\left( \frac{M_1-M_2}{M_2}\right) (100)\\-\left( \frac{V_i-V_f}{M_2}\right) (\rho_w)(100)}$$

Shrinkage Ratio
$$\displaylines{SR=\frac{M_2}{V_f\rho_w}}$$

Specific Gravity
$$\displaylines{G_s=\frac{1}{\frac{1}{SR}-\left(\frac{SL}{100}\right)}}$$

Volumetric Shrinkage
$$\displaylines{VS(\%)=SR[\omega (\%)-SL]}$$

Linear Shrinkage
$$\displaylines{LS(\%)=100\left[1-\left(\frac{100}{VS(\%)+100} \right)^{\frac{1}{3}} \right]}$$

Chapter - 8

Flow through a Two-layered Soil
$$\displaylines{i=\frac{h_1}{H_1+H_2}\\\\\textsf{For }0\le z\le H_1\;,\\h=h_1\left(1-\frac{k_2z}{k_1H_2+k_2H_1}\right)\\\\\textsf{For }H_1\le z\le H_1+H_2\;,\\h=h_1\left[\left(\frac{k_1}{k_1H_2+k_2H_1} \right)(H_1+H_2-z) \right] \\\\z\textsf{ not given, }h_2=\frac{h_1k_1}{H_1\left(\frac{k_1}{H_1}+\frac{k_2}{H_2} \right)} }$$

Flow Net
$$\displaylines{\Delta H=\frac{H_1-H_2}{N_d}\\q=\frac{k(H_1-H_2)N_f}{N_d}\\q=\textsf{the total rate of flow through all channels}\\H=\textsf{head difference}\\N_d=\textsf{number of potential drops}\\N_f=\textsf{number of flow channels}\\Q=qL=\frac{q}{kH}\times kHL}$$

$$\displaylines{\textsf{Page 260 - figure}\\\Delta =\frac{H}{\tan\beta}\\d=0.3\Delta + \frac{h-H}{\tan\beta}+B+\frac{h}{\tan\alpha}\\L=\frac{d}{\cos\alpha}-\sqrt{\frac{d^2}{\cos^2\alpha}-\frac{H^2}{\sin^2\alpha}}}$$
