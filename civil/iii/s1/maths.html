10.1 - Sequences

Convergence or Divergence
$$\displaylines{\textsf{(Sequences, Sandwich, Integral test)}\\\textsf{Constant}\rightarrow \textsf{Convergence}\\\pm\infty,\textsf{ limit doesn't exist}\rightarrow \textsf{Divergence}}$$

$$\displaylines{\textsf{trigo}^1\rightarrow -1\le \textsf{trigo}\le 1\\\textsf{trigo}^2\rightarrow 0\le \textsf{trigo}\le 1}$$

T - 5
$$\displaylines{\#1\lim_{n\to\infty}{\ln n\over n} = 0\quad\#2\lim_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{n}=1\\ \#3\lim_{n\to\infty}x^{\frac{1}{n}}=1\,(x\gt 0)\\\#4\lim_{n\to\infty}x^n=0\,(|x|\lt 1)\\\#5\lim_{n\to\infty}{(1+{x\over n})^n}=e^x \,(\textsf{any }x)\\\#6\lim_{n\to\infty}{x^n\over n!}=0\,(\textsf{any }x)}$$

10.2 - Infinite Series

Infinite Geometric Series
$$\displaylines{S={a\over{1-r}}\quad|r|\lt 1\rightarrow \textsf{Convergence}\\|r|\ge 1\rightarrow \textsf{Divergence}\\\sum_{n=1}^\infty ar^{n-1}\textsf{ (or) }\sum_{n=0}^\infty ar^n}$$

Telescoping Series
$$\displaylines{\sum_{n=1}^\infty (b_n-b_{n+1})=(b_1-b_2)+(b_2-b_3)+...\\+(b_n-b_{n-1})+...+(b_k-b_{k+1})\\\\k^{th}\textsf{ partial sum, }S_k=b_1-b_{k+1}\\\textsf{Notes: }\tan^{-1}\infty = \frac{\pi}{2},\;\tan^{-1}1=\frac{\pi}{4}}$$

10.3 - Integral Test

Integral Test and p-series
$$\displaylines{\textsf{Integral test}\quad\int_1^\infty f(x)dx\\\textsf{Constant}\rightarrow\textsf{Convergence}\\\pm\infty,\;\textsf{limit doesn't exist}\rightarrow\textsf{Divergence}\\\\\textsf{p-series }({1\over n^p})\quad p\gt 1\rightarrow \textsf{Convergence}\\p\le 1\rightarrow \textsf{Divergence}}$$

10.4 - Comparison Test

T - 10
$$\displaylines{d_n\le a_n\le c_n\\\textsf{if }\Sigma c_n\textsf{ is con;, }\Sigma a_n\textsf{ is con;}\\\textsf{if }\Sigma d_n\textsf{ is div;, }\Sigma a_n\textsf{ is div;}}$$

T - 11 Limit Comparison Test
$$\displaylines{ 1.\,\lim_{n\to\infty} \frac{a_n}{b_n}=c\gt 0\\\textsf{both }\Sigma a_n\textsf{ and }\Sigma b_n\textsf{ con; (or) div;}\\ 2.\,\lim_{n\to\infty} \frac{a_n}{b_n}=0\\\textsf{if }\Sigma b_n\textsf{ is con;, }\Sigma a_n\textsf{ is con;}\\ 3.\,\lim_{n\to\infty} \frac{a_n}{b_n}=\infty\\\textsf{if }\Sigma b_n\textsf{ is div;, }\Sigma a_n\textsf{ is div;} }$$

10.5 Ratio Test

Ratio Test and Root Test
$$\displaylines{\rho\lt 1\rightarrow \textsf{Convergence}\\\rho\gt 1 \rightarrow \textsf{Divergence}\\\rho =1\rightarrow \textsf{The test is inconclusive}}$$

T - 12 Ratio Test
$$\displaylines{\textsf{Ratio test}\rightarrow\rho = \lim_{n\to\infty}{a_{n+1}\over a_n}\\\textsf{Root test}\rightarrow\rho = \lim_{n\to\infty}{\sqrt[n]{a_n}} \\\lim_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{c}=1 \\\lim_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{n^p}=1 \\\lim_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{\ln n}=1 \\\lim_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{n!}=\infty \\\lim_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{n}=1}$$

6.1 - Laplace Transform. Linearity

First Shifting Theorem
$$\displaylines{F(s)=\mathscr{L}(f)=\int_0^\infty e^{-st}f(t)dt\\1\rightarrow {1\over s}\quad t\rightarrow {1\over s^2}\quad t^2\rightarrow {\frac{2!}{s^3}}\quad t^n\rightarrow \frac{n!}{s^{n+1}}\\ e^{at}\rightarrow \frac{1}{s-a}\quad t^ne^{at}\rightarrow \frac{n!}{(s-a)^{n+1}}\\ \cos\omega t\rightarrow \frac{s}{s^2+\omega^2}\quad \sin\omega t\rightarrow \frac{\omega}{s^2+\omega^2}\\ \cosh\omega t\rightarrow \frac{s}{s^2-\omega^2}\quad \sinh\omega t\rightarrow \frac{\omega}{s^2-\omega^2}\\ e^{at}\cos\omega t\rightarrow \frac{(s-a)}{(s-a)^2+\omega^2}\\ e^{at}\sin\omega t\rightarrow \frac{\omega}{(s-a)^2+\omega^2}\\\mathscr{L}^{-1}(F)=f(t)}$$

Chapter - 5 (Fourier Analysis)

11.1

Fourier Series
$$\displaylines{a_0=\frac{1}{2\pi}\int_{-\pi}^\pi f(x)dx\\a_n=\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{-\pi}^\pi f(x)\cos nx\;dx\\b_n=\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{-\pi}^\pi f(x)\sin nx\;dx\\\textsf{The Fourier series is}\\f(x)=a_0+\sum_{n=1}^\infty (a_n\cos nx+b_n\sin nx)}$$

11.2

From Period 2\(\pi\) to Any Period \(p=2L\)
$$\displaylines{a_0=\frac{1}{2L}\int_{-L}^Lf(x)dx\quad p=2L\\a_n=\frac{1}{L}\int_{-L}^Lf(x)\cos \frac{n\pi x}{L}dx\\b_n=\frac{1}{L}\int_{-L}^Lf(x)\sin\frac{n\pi x}{L}dx\\\textsf{The Fourier series is}\\f(x)=a_0+\sum_{n=1}^\infty\left(a_n\cos\frac{n\pi x}{L}+b_n\sin\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right)}$$
