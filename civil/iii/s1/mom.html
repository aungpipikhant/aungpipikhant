Chapter - 1 (Stress)

Normal Stress
$$\sigma_{\textsf{avg}}=\frac{N}{A}$$

Shear Stress
$$\tau_{\textsf{avg}}=\frac{V}{A}$$

Bearing Stress
$$\displaylines{\textsf{Contact area between 2 members}\\\sigma_{b}=\frac{\textsf{compression}}{\textsf{area}}}$$

ASD (Allowable Stress Design)
$$\displaylines{\textsf{Factor of safety, }F.S=\frac{\sigma_{fail}}{\sigma_{all}}\\A=\frac{F}{\sigma_{all}}\quad \sigma_{all}=\frac{\sigma_{fail}}{F.S}}$$

LRFD (Load & Resistance Factor Design)
$$\displaylines{\textsf{Dead load, Live load, Snow load}\\R_i=1.2D+1.6L+0.5S\\\phi P_n\ge P_u\quad \phi M_n\ge M_u\\P_u=\Sigma r_iR_i\quad P_n=\sigma_{fail}\times A}$$

Chapter - 3

Mechanical Properties of Materials
$$\displaylines{\sigma = \frac{P}{A_0}\quad\varepsilon = \frac{\delta}{L_0}\quad \delta = \sum \varepsilon L\\\textsf{Hooke's law}\rightarrow\sigma = E\varepsilon\quad\varepsilon =\frac{\sigma}{E}}$$

Chapter - 4 (Axial Load)

Displacement
$$\displaylines{\delta=\frac{PL}{AE}\\P=\textsf{Axial load}\\L=\textsf{Length}\\A=\textsf{x-section area}\\E=\textsf{Elasticity (or) Elastic modulus}\\\textsf{Volume of cone}=\frac{1}{3}\pi R^2h\\\textsf{Specific weight, }\gamma =\frac{W}{V} \\\delta = \int_0^L\frac{P_{(y)}dy}{A_{(y)}E} }$$

Thermal stress
$$\displaylines{\delta_T=\alpha (\Delta T)L\\\alpha=\textsf{Linear coefficient of thermal expansion}\\\textsf{(Table in last 2 pages).}\\(\Delta T)=\textsf{Change in temperature}\\L=\textsf{Original length}}$$

Compatibility
$$\displaylines{\delta_{\textsf{material 1}}=\delta_{\textsf{material 2}}\\\delta_{\textsf{material}}=(\delta_{\textsf{material}})_T-(\delta_{\textsf{material}})_F}$$

Chapter - 5

Torsion
$$\displaylines{\tau = \frac{Tc}{J}\\T=\textsf{Torque or twisting moment}\\c = \textsf{Distance from NA to considered point}\\J = \textsf{Polar moment of inertia}\\\textsf{Shaft/Solid},\;J=\frac{\pi}{2}R^4\\\textsf{Tube},\;J=\frac{\pi}{2}(R_o^4-R_i^4)\\ R_o\textsf{ is outer radius.}\\R_i\textsf{ is inner radius.}}$$

Angle of Twist
$$\displaylines{\phi = \sum\frac{TL}{JG}\\G=\textsf{Shear modulus of elasticity}\\L=\textsf{Length}}$$

Displacement of tooth P on gear A
$$\displaylines{s_P=\phi_Ar}$$

Chapter - 6 (Bending)

Axial load
$$\displaylines{\textsf{Tension}\rightarrow +\\\textsf{Compression}\rightarrow -}$$

L.D to S.F.D
$$\displaylines{\textsf{no load}\rightarrow \textsf{straight line}\\\textsf{point load}\rightarrow \textsf{jump (or) drop}\\\textsf{U.D load}\rightarrow \textsf{decreasing/increasing straight line}\\\textsf{varying load}\rightarrow \textsf{curve}}$$

S.F.D to B.M.D (Area of S.F.D)
$$\displaylines{\textsf{no shear}\rightarrow \textsf{straight line}\\\textsf{moment}\rightarrow \;\circlearrowright +\textsf{ (or) }\circlearrowleft -\quad\uparrow\downarrow\\\textsf{U.D shear}\rightarrow\textsf{decreasing/increasing straight line}\\\textsf{variable shear}\rightarrow\textsf{curve}}$$

Finding \(x\) distance
$$\displaylines{\begin{align}&1.\quad\textsf{Section at }x\\&2.\quad\textsf{Find varying/point loads}\\&3.\quad\textsf{Find }\sum F_y\textsf{ and }\sum M\end{align}}$$

Centroid
$$\displaylines{\bar{y}=\frac{\sum \bar{y}A}{\sum A}\\\textsf{for rectangle, }I_{xx}=I_o=\frac{bh^3}{12}\\I_{yy}=I_o=\frac{hb^3}{12}\\\textsf{for circle, }I_{xx}=I_{yy}=\frac{\pi d^2}{64}\\\textsf{for triangle, }I_{xx}=I_o=\frac{bh^3}{36} \\\textsf{Asymmetric, }I=I_o+Ak^2\textsf{ (where }k=\bar{y}-y\textsf{)}}$$
