M/D/1 Queuing
$$\displaylines{ \rho =\frac{\lambda}{\mu}\quad\quad\bar{Q}=\frac{\rho^2}{2(1-\rho)}\\\\\bar{w}=\frac{\rho}{2\mu (1-\rho)}\quad\quad\bar{t}=\frac{2-\rho}{2\mu (1-\rho)}\\\\\rho\lt 1\\\rho=\textsf{traffic intensity}\\\lambda=\textsf{average arrival rate (veh/min)}\\\mu=\textsf{average departure rate (veh/min)}\\\bar{Q}=\textsf{average length of queue (veh)}\\\bar{w}=\textsf{average waiting time (min/veh)}\\\bar{t}=\textsf{average time spent (min/veh)} }$$

M/M/1 Queuing
$$\displaylines{ \bar{Q}=\frac{\rho^2}{1-\rho}\quad\quad\bar{w}=\frac{\lambda}{\mu(\mu - \lambda)}\\\\\bar{t}=\frac{1}{\mu - \lambda} }$$

M/M/N Queuing
$$\displaylines{ \rho\gt 1\\\\P_0=\frac{1}{\sum\limits_{n_c=0}^{N-1}\frac{\rho^{n_c}}{n_c!} + \frac{\rho^N}{N!(1-\rho / N)} }\\\\P_{n\gt N}=\frac{P_0\space\rho^{N+1}}{N!N(1-\rho /N)} \\\\\bar{Q}=\frac{P_0\space\rho^{N+1}}{N!N}\left[\frac{1}{(1-\rho /N)^2}\right]\\\\\bar{t}=\frac{\rho + \bar{Q}}{\lambda} }$$
