16.3

Residue Integration Method
$$\displaylines{ \begin{align} f(z)&=\sum_{n=0}^\infty a_n(z-z_0)^n+\sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{b_n}{(z-z_0)^n}\\ &=a_0+a_1(z-z_0)+a_2(z-z_0)^2+...\\ &+\frac{b_1}{(z-z_0)}+\frac{b_2}{(z-z_0)^2}+\frac{b_3}{(z-z_0)^3}+... \end{align}\\\\ b_1=\mathop{Res}_{z=z_0}\space f(z)\\ \oint f(z)dz=2\pi i\space b_1\quad\textsf{(C.C.W)}\\ \oint f(z)dz=-2\pi i\space b_1\quad\textsf{(C.W)} }$$

Residue at a Simple Pole
$$\displaylines{ \mathop{Res}_{z=z_0}\space f(z)=\lim_{z\to z_0}(z-z_0)f(z)\\\mathop{Res}_{z=z_0}\space f(z)=\mathop{Res}_{z=z_0}\space\frac{p(z)}{q(z)}=\frac{p(z)}{q'(z)} }$$

Residue at a Pole of Higher order
$$\displaylines{ \mathop{Res}_{z=z_0}\space f(z)=\\\frac{1}{(m-1)!}\lim_{z\to z_0}\left[\frac{d^{m-1}}{dz^{m-1}}(z-z_0)^m f(z)\right] }$$

Residue Theorem
$$\displaylines{ \textsf{(C.C.W)}\quad\oint f(z)dz=2\pi i\sum_{j=1}^k\mathop{Res}_{z=z_j}\space f(z)\\ \textsf{(C.W)}\quad\oint f(z)dz=-2\pi i\sum_{j=1}^k\mathop{Res}_{z=z_j}\space f(z) }$$
