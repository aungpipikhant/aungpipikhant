Triaxial Shear Test

Stresses
$$\displaylines{ \sigma_3=\Biggl\{\quad\displaylines{\textsf{Minor principal stress}\\\textsf{All round pressure}\\\textsf{Chamber confining pressure}}\\\\ \Delta\sigma_d=\biggl\{\quad\displaylines{\textsf{Deviator stress}\\\textsf{Principal shear difference}}\\\\ \sigma_1=\biggl\{\quad\displaylines{\textsf{Major principal stress}\\\textsf{Axial stress}}\\\\ \sigma_1=\sigma_3+\Delta\sigma_d }$$

Consolidated-Drained Triaxial Test \((\sigma=\sigma ')\)

Notes
$$\displaylines{ \textsf{Minor principal stress, }\sigma_3=\sigma_3 '\\\textsf{Major principal stress, }\sigma_1=\sigma_1 '=\sigma_3 '+(\Delta\sigma_d)_f\\\\\sin\phi '=\frac{\left(\frac{\sigma_1 '-\sigma_3 '}{2}\right)}{c'\cot\phi '+\left(\frac{\sigma_1 '+\sigma_3 '}{2}\right)}\\\\\sigma_1 '=\sigma_3 '\tan^2(45+\frac{\phi '}{2})+2c'\tan(45+\frac{\phi '}{2})\\\textsf{Failure plane, }\theta =45+\frac{\phi '}{2}\\\textsf{Maximum shear stress, }\theta =45^\circ }$$

At Failure Plane
$$\displaylines{ \sigma_f=\frac{\sigma_1 '+\sigma_3 '}{2}+\frac{\sigma_1 '-\sigma_3 '}{2}\space\cos 2\theta\\\\\tau_f=\left(\frac{\sigma_1 '-\sigma_3 '}{2}\right)\space\sin 2\theta\\\\\textsf{Sandy soil, sand, normally consolidated clay}\\\Rightarrow c=0 }$$

Consolidated-Undrained Triaxial Test

Major Principal Stress at Failure
$$\displaylines{ \textsf{Total:}\quad\sigma_1=\sigma_3+(\Delta\sigma_d)_f\\ \textsf{Effective:}\quad\sigma_1 '=\sigma_1-(\Delta u_d)_f\\(\Delta u_d)_f=\textsf{pore water pressure at failure} }$$

Minor Principal Stress at Failure
$$\displaylines{ \textsf{Total:}\quad\sigma_3\\ \textsf{Effective:}\quad\sigma_3 '=\sigma_3-(\Delta u_d)_f }$$

$$\displaylines{ \phi=\sin^{-1}\left(\frac{\sigma_1-\sigma_3}{\sigma_1+\sigma_3}\right)\\\\ \phi '=\sin^{-1}\left(\frac{\sigma_1 '-\sigma_3 '}{\sigma_1 '+\sigma_3 '}\right) }$$
