Porosity
$$\displaylines{ n=\frac{V_v}{V_{\textsf{bulk}}}\times 100\% }$$

Density
$$\displaylines{ \rho_{\textsf{bulk}}=\frac{M_{\textsf{bulk}}}{V_{\textsf{bulk}}} }$$

Pore Volume
$$\displaylines{ V_v=\frac{M_{\textsf{sat}}-M_{\textsf{dry}}}{\rho_w}\\\\V_{\textsf{bulk}}=\pi r^2h\quad (or)\quad \frac{M_{\textsf{sat}}-M_{\textsf{sub}}}{\rho_w} }$$

Standard Deviation
$$\displaylines{ S^2=\frac{\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\; (x_i-\bar{x})^2}{n-1}\\\textsf{Coefficient of variation of the result}=\frac{S}{\bar{x}} }$$

Water Content
$$\displaylines{ \omega =\frac{M_{\textsf{bulk}}-M_{\textsf{dry}}}{M_{\textsf{dry}}}\times 100\% }$$

Brazilian Tensile Test
$$\displaylines{ \sigma_t\approx\frac{2F}{\pi DH}\\\\1\; kN=101.9784\; kgF\\1\; MPa=10.2\; kgF/cm^2 }$$

Triaxial Compression Test
$$\displaylines{ \sigma_1=\frac{\textsf{Maximum Load, P}}{\textsf{Area, A}} }$$

Mohr-Coulomb Friction Law
$$\displaylines{ \sigma_1=\frac{2c\cos\phi + \sigma_3(1+\sin\phi)}{1-\sin\phi}\\\\\textsf{(or)}\\\\\sigma_1=\frac{2c\cos\phi}{1-\sin\phi}+\frac{\sigma_3(1+\sin\phi)}{1-\sin\phi}\\\\\phi=\textsf{angle of internal friction}\\c=\textsf{cohesion of intact rock}\\\\\textsf{Incremental strength due to}\\\textsf{confining pressure \(\sigma_3\) is}\\\sigma_1\rightarrow\sigma_1+\Delta\sigma_1\\\sigma_3\rightarrow\sigma_3+\Delta\sigma_3 }$$

Uniaxial Compressive Strength
$$\displaylines{ \textsf{When \(\sigma_3=0\) ,}\quad\sigma_c=2c\; \frac{\cos\phi}{1-\sin\phi} }$$

Hoek and Brown Strength Parameters
$$\displaylines{ m=\frac{BC}{AB}\\\\S=1\; (\textsf{app})\quad\quad (\textsf{0 to A})\\\\\textsf{Using Hoek and Brown yield criterion,}\\\sigma_1=\sigma_3+[\; m\sigma_c\sigma_3+S\; \sigma_c^2\; ]^{0.5} }$$
