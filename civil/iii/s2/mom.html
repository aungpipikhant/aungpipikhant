Chapter - 9

Stress Transformation

General Equations
$$\displaylines{ \sigma_{x'}=\frac{\sigma_x+\sigma_y}{2}+\frac{\sigma_x-\sigma_y}{2}\space\cos 2\theta + \tau_{xy}\sin 2\theta\\\\ \sigma_{y'}=\frac{\sigma_x+\sigma_y}{2}-\frac{\sigma_x-\sigma_y}{2}\space\cos 2\theta - \tau_{xy}\sin 2\theta\\\\ \tau_{x'y'}=-\frac{\sigma_x-\sigma_y}{2}\space\sin 2\theta + \tau_{xy}\cos 2\theta }$$

Principal Stresses
$$\displaylines{ \tan 2\theta_p=\frac{\tau_{xy}}{(\sigma_x-\sigma_y)/2}\\\\ \sigma_{1,2}=\frac{\sigma_x+\sigma_y}{2}\pm \sqrt{\left(\frac{\sigma_x-\sigma_y}{2}\right)^2+\tau_{xy}^2} }$$

Maximum In-Plane Shear Stress
$$\displaylines{ \tan 2\theta_s=\frac{-(\sigma_x-\sigma_y)/2}{\tau_{xy}}\\\\ \sigma_{\textsf{avg}}=\frac{\sigma_x+\sigma_y}{2}\\\\ \tau_{\displaylines{\textsf{max}\\\textsf{in-plane}}}=\sqrt{\left(\frac{\sigma_x-\sigma_y}{2}\right)^2+\tau_{xy}^2} }$$

Mohr's Circle
$$\displaylines{ R=\sqrt{\left(\frac{\sigma_x-\sigma_y}{2}\right)^2+\tau_{xy}^2}\\\textsf{(or)}\\R=\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\\\\ \textsf{Centre C}=(\sigma_{\textsf{avg}},\space 0)\\ \textsf{Point A}=(\sigma_x,\space \tau_{xy}) }$$

Notes
$$\displaylines{ 2\theta_{p_1}\textsf{ and }2\theta_{p_2}\textsf{ are 180° apart in Mohr's Circle.}\\ 2\theta_{s_1}\textsf{ and }2\theta_{s_2}\textsf{ are 180° apart in Mohr's Circle.}\\ 2\theta_{p}\textsf{ and }2\theta_{s}\textsf{ are 90° apart in Mohr's Circle.} }$$

Chapter - 10

Strain Transformation

General Equations
$$\displaylines{ \varepsilon_{x'}=\frac{\varepsilon_x+\varepsilon_y}{2}+\frac{\varepsilon_x-\varepsilon_y}{2}\space\cos 2\theta + \frac{\gamma_{xy}}{2}\sin 2\theta\\\\ \varepsilon_{y'}=\frac{\varepsilon_x+\varepsilon_y}{2}-\frac{\varepsilon_x-\varepsilon_y}{2}\space\cos 2\theta - \frac{\gamma_{xy}}{2}\sin 2\theta\\\\ \frac{\gamma_{x'y'}}{2}=-\left(\frac{\varepsilon_x-\varepsilon_y}{2}\right)\space\sin 2\theta + \frac{\gamma_{xy}}{2}\cos 2\theta }$$

Principal Strains
$$\displaylines{ \tan 2\theta_p=\frac{\gamma_{xy}}{\varepsilon_x-\varepsilon_y}\\\\ \varepsilon_{1,2}=\frac{\varepsilon_x+\varepsilon_y}{2}\pm \sqrt{\left(\frac{\varepsilon_x-\varepsilon_y}{2}\right)^2+\left(\frac{\gamma_{xy}}{2}\right)^2} }$$

Maximum In-Plane Shear Strain
$$\displaylines{ \tan 2\theta_s=-\left(\frac{\varepsilon_x-\varepsilon_y}{\gamma_{xy}}\right)\\\\ \varepsilon_{\textsf{avg}}=\frac{\varepsilon_x+\varepsilon_y}{2}\\\\ \frac{\gamma_{\displaylines{\textsf{max}\\\textsf{in-plane}}}}{2}=\sqrt{\left(\frac{\varepsilon_x-\varepsilon_y}{2}\right)^2+\left(\frac{\gamma_{xy}}{2}\right)^2} }$$

Mohr's Circle
$$\displaylines{ R=\sqrt{\left(\frac{\varepsilon_x-\varepsilon_y}{2}\right)^2+\left(\frac{\gamma_{xy}}{2}\right)^2}\\\textsf{(or)}\\R=\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\\\\ \textsf{Centre C}=(\varepsilon_{\textsf{avg}},\space 0)\\ \textsf{Point A}=(\varepsilon_x,\space \frac{\gamma_{xy}}{2}) }$$

Generalized Hooke's Law
$$\displaylines{ \varepsilon_x=\frac{1}{E}[\;\sigma_x-\nu (\sigma_y+\sigma_z)\; ]\\ \varepsilon_y=\frac{1}{E}[\;\sigma_y-\nu (\sigma_x+\sigma_z)\; ]\\ \varepsilon_z=\frac{1}{E}[\;\sigma_z-\nu (\sigma_x+\sigma_y)\; ] }$$

Dilatation
$$\displaylines{ e=\frac{1-2\nu}{E}(\sigma_x+ \sigma_y+ \sigma_z) }$$
