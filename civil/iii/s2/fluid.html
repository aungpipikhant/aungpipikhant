Steady Incompressible Flow in Pipe and Duct Systems

Steady Flow Energy Equation Between A and B
$$\displaylines{ \frac{p_A}{\rho g}+\frac{v_A^2}{2g}+Z_A=\frac{p_B}{\rho g}+\frac{v_B^2}{2g}+Z_B+\textsf{Losses}\\\\ \textsf{Pressure, }p=0\quad\textsf{(\(\because\) Atmospheric pressure)}\\\textsf{Velocity, }v=0\quad\textsf{(\(\because\) Reservior is large)}\\\textsf{Minor loss of exit}=0\quad\textsf{(\(\because\) Atmospheric pressure)}\\\\\textsf{Loss of head at entrance, }h=\frac{0.5v^2}{2g}\\\\\textsf{Fraction loss, }h_f=\frac{4flv^2}{2gd}\\\\\textsf{Loss of head at sudden enlargement,}\\h=\frac{(v_1-v_2)^2}{2g}\\\\\textsf{Loss of head at exit, }h=\frac{v^2}{2g} }$$

Duct Systems
$$\displaylines{ \frac{p_A}{\rho g}+\frac{v_A^2}{2g}+Z_A+\frac{\Delta p_{\textsf{fan}}}{\rho g}\\=\\\frac{p_B}{\rho g}+\frac{v_B^2}{2g}+Z_B+\sum\textsf{Losses}\\\\ \Delta p_{\textsf{fan}}=\sum\left[\frac{\rho Kv^2}{2}\right]\\\\ \textsf{Friction loss}=\rho g\left(\frac{4flv^2}{2gd}\right)=\frac{4\rho flQ^2}{2dA^2}\\\\ \begin{align} \textsf{Friction loss (rectanglar)}&=\rho g\left(\frac{flv^2}{2gm}\right)\\\\&=\frac{\rho flv^2}{2m}\\\\&=\frac{\rho flQ^2}{2mA^2} \end{align}\\\\m=\frac{A}{P}\\\\ \textsf{Loss of transition}=\frac{4\rho fl_eQ^2}{2dA^2}\\\\ \textsf{Entry loss}=\frac{\rho Kv^2}{2}=\frac{\rho K_{\textsf{entry}}Q^2}{2A^2}\\\\ \textsf{Loss of exit grille}=\frac{\rho Kv^2}{2}=\frac{\rho K_{\textsf{exit}}Q^2}{2A^2}\\\\l_e=\textsf{equivalent length separation loss} }$$
