Chapter - 3+4

Total Energy
$$\displaylines{ E=y+\frac{V^2}{2g} }$$

Discharge
$$\displaylines{ Q=AV\,,\quad q=\frac{Q}{b} }$$

Froud Number
$$\displaylines{ Fr=\frac{V}{\sqrt{gD}}\\Fr\gt 1\Rightarrow\textsf{supercritical flow}\\Fr\lt 1\Rightarrow\textsf{subcritical flow} }$$

Specific Force
$$\displaylines{ F=\frac{Q^2}{gA}+\bar{z}A=\frac{Q^2}{g\,(by)}+\frac{y}{2}(by) }$$

Notes
$$\displaylines{ \textsf{For critical flow}\Rightarrow \alpha\,\frac{V^2}{2g}=\frac{D}{2}\\\\\textsf{At critical state, rectangular channel}\\y_c=\sqrt[3]{\frac{q^2}{g}}\\\\\textsf{At critical state of flow,}\\\textsf{the specific energy is minimum.}\\\frac{dE}{dy}=0\\\\\textsf{For minimum specific force, }\frac{dF}{dy}=0\\\\\textsf{For maximum discharge, }\frac{dQ}{dy}=0\\\\E_c=y_c+\alpha\,\frac{V_c^2}{2\,g}\textsf{ for all channels} }$$

Channels
$$\displaylines{ A=TD\quad\quad D=\frac{A}{T}\\\textsf{T = Top width}\quad\quad\textsf{P = Wetted perimeter}\\\\\textsf{For side slope z:1 (1 on z) },\\\\\textsf{Rectangular}\Rightarrow A=by\quad\; T=b\quad\; D=y\\P=b+2y\\\\\textsf{Triangular}\Rightarrow A=\frac{1}{2}Ty\quad\quad T=2zy\\ P=2y\,\sqrt{1+z^2}\\\\\textsf{Trapezoidal}\Rightarrow A=\frac{(b+T)}{2}\;y=(b+zy)\,y\\T=b+2zy\quad\quad P=b+2y\,\sqrt{1+z^2}\\\\\textsf{Hydraulic radius, }R=\frac{A}{P} }$$

Section Factor
$$\displaylines{ Z=A\sqrt{D}\quad\quad Z=\frac{Q_c}{\sqrt{g}} }$$

Channel Transition
$$\displaylines{ \textsf{Contraction/Expansion}\Rightarrow E_1=E_2\\\textsf{Upward Step}\Rightarrow E_1=E_2+\Delta_z\\\textsf{Downward Step}\Rightarrow E_1=E_2 -\Delta_z\\\\\textsf{Change in depth, }\Delta y=y_2-y_1\\\\\textsf{Change in elevation}\\\textsf{Upward step}\Rightarrow (y_2+\Delta_z)-y_1\\\textsf{Downward step}\Rightarrow y_2-(y_1+\Delta_z)\\\\E_c=\frac{3}{2}y_c\quad\quad V_c=\sqrt{gy_c}\\\\\textsf{Greatest allowable contraction in}\\\textsf{width for the upstream flow,}\\E_1=E_c\\\\\textsf{Maximum allowable size of upward step}\\\textsf{for the upstream flow,}\\E_1=E_c+\Delta_{zc}\\\\y_c=\sqrt[3]{\frac{Q^2}{g\,b^2}} }$$

Chapter - 5

Development of Uniform Flow and Its Formulas

The Manning Formula
$$\displaylines{ V=\frac{1.49}{n}\,R^{\frac{2}{3}}\,S^{\frac{1}{2}}\\n=\textsf{Manning's n} }$$

The Chezy Formula
$$\displaylines{ V=C\sqrt{RS}\\V=\textsf{mean velocity}\\R=\textsf{hydraulic radius}\\S=\textsf{slope of energy line}\\C=\textsf{Chezy's coefficient} }$$

The G.K Formula / Kutter's Formula
$$\displaylines{ C=\frac{41.65+\frac{0.00281}{S}+\frac{1.811}{n}}{1+(41.65+\frac{0.00281}{S})\,\frac{n}{\sqrt{R}}}\\\\n=\textsf{coefficient of roughness (Kutter's n)} }$$

The Bazin Formula
$$\displaylines{ C=\frac{157.6}{1+\frac{m}{\sqrt{R}}}\\\\m=\textsf{roughness coefficient (Bazin's m)}\\m\Rightarrow\textsf{Table 5.1 Page 95} }$$

The Powell Formula
$$\displaylines{ C=-42\log (\frac{C}{4\,速}+\frac{\epsilon}{R})\\\\\epsilon \,=\textsf{roughness coefficient (Powell's \(\epsilon\) ) }\\\epsilon\Rightarrow\textsf{Table 5.3 Page 98}\\速=\textsf{Reynolds number}\\\textsf{For rough channel, }C=42\log (\frac{R}{\epsilon})\\\textsf{For smooth channel, }C=42\log (\frac{4\,速}{C})\\\\速=\frac{VR}{\nu}\\\textsf{Kinematic viscosity of water, }\nu=1.08\times 10^{-5} }$$

Chapter - 6

Computation of Uniform Flow

The Conveyance of a Channel Section, K
$$\displaylines{ K=\frac{Q}{\sqrt{S}}\\\\\textsf{When the Chezy's formula is used,}\\K=CAR^{\frac{1}{2}}\\\\\textsf{When the Manning's Formula is used,}\\K=\frac{1.49}{n}\,AR^{\frac{2}{3}}\\\\AR^{\frac{2}{3}}=\frac{nK}{1.49}\\\\AR^{\frac{2}{3}}=\frac{n}{1.49}\,\frac{Q}{\sqrt{S}}\\\\Q=\frac{1.49}{n}\,AR^{\frac{2}{3}}\,S^{\frac{1}{2}}\\\\V=\frac{1.49}{n}\,R^{\frac{2}{3}}\,S^{\frac{1}{2}} }$$

Notes
$$\displaylines{ V=\sqrt{gD}\quad\quad\frac{D}{2}=\frac{V^2}{2\,g}\\\\A=TD\quad\quad D=\frac{A}{T}\quad\quad\frac{Q^2}{gA^2}=\frac{A}{T} }$$

The Velocity-Distribution Coefficients
$$\displaylines{ \alpha =\frac{\sum_1^N\;(\alpha_N\,K_N^3\,/\,\Delta A_N^2)}{(\sum_1^N\;K_N)^3\,/\,A^2}\\\\ \beta =\frac{\sum_1^N\;(\beta_N\,K_N^2\,/\,\Delta A_N)}{(\sum_1^N\;K_N)^2\,/\,A} }$$

The Equivalent Coefficient of Roughness
$$\displaylines{ n=\left[\,\frac{\sum_1^N\,(P_N\,n_N^{1.5})}{P}\,\right]^{\frac{2}{3}}\\\\n=\frac{(P_1\,n_1^{1.5}+P_2\,n_2^{1.5}+...+P_N\,n_N^{1.5})^{\frac{2}{3}}}{P^{\frac{2}{3}}} }$$
