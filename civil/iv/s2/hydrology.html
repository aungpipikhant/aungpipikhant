Groundwater

Confined / Artesian / Leaky Aquifer
$$\displaylines{ T=KB\quad\quad H=s+h\\\\ T\Rightarrow\textsf{Transmissivity}\\ K\Rightarrow\textsf{Coefficient of permeability}\\ B\Rightarrow\textsf{Length/Thickness of strainer}\\ s\Rightarrow\textsf{Drawdown}\\\\ Q=\frac{2\,\pi\,T\,(h_2-h_1)}{\ln\frac{r_2}{r_1}}=\frac{2\,\pi\,KB\,(h_2-h_1)}{\ln\frac{r_2}{r_1}}\\\\ Q=\frac{2\,\pi\,T\,(s_1-s_2)}{\ln\frac{r_2}{r_1}}=\frac{2\,\pi\,KB\,(s_1-s_2)}{\ln\frac{r_2}{r_1}}\\\\ \textsf{At the edge of the zone of influence}\,,\\s_1=s_w\,,\,s_2=0\,,\,r_1=r_w\,,\,r_2=R\\h_1=h_w\,,\,h_2=H\\\\ \textsf{At pumping well}\,,\\ Q=\frac{2\,\pi\,T\,s_w}{\ln\frac{R}{r_w}}\\\\ \textsf{\(T\) and \(s_w\) are constant}\,, \frac{Q_1}{Q_2}=\frac{\ln\frac{R}{r_{w_2}}}{\ln\frac{R}{r_{w_1}}}\\\\ \textsf{\(Q \varpropto s_w\) for constant \(T\,,\,R\) and \(r_w\)}\,,\\\\ \frac{Q_1}{Q_2}=\frac{s_{w_1}}{s_{w_2}} }$$
